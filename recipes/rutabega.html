Rutabega Cloud

Description

Steamed rutabega pureed with vegan butter and plant milk makes a fluffy cloud of deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 pounds rutabegas, whole or quartered, depending on size
  • 1-2 tablespoon plant milk
  • 1-2 tablespoons vegan butter

Steps

  1. Steam rutabegas in a pressure cooker or on the stove top until very tender.
  2. Carefully slip the skins off of the steamed rutabegas and return them to the pot.
  3. Add plant milk and vegan butter to the rutabegas and puree with an imersion blender. Add more liquid as needed.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and serve hot.
