Vegan Cesar Dressing

Description

Creamy vegan cesar dressing that is ready in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vegan yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon tamari
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons capers + 1/2 teaspoon of their brine
  • 1 teasoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a jar or measuring jug and blend with an immersion blender.
  2. Serve as a dressing for cesar salad or use as a dip!

Adapted from Loving It Vegan's Vegan Cesar Dressing.
